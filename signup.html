FAQs

How do I upgrade?

Upgrading from a free or premium membership is easily done at anytime during your subcription right from the dashboard of your CARDS app. Simply click the upgrade button under the settings panel and choose the new plan you would like to upgrade to.

How do monthly subscriptions work?

We only require monthly commitments at a time. Your subscription is automatically renewed after one month, unless you cancel before the next scheduled billing date. Should your subscription be cancelled, you will automatically be downgraded to the free account. Your personally made content content will still be accessable to you.

Can I cancel at any time?

Plans can be cancelled at anytime in from the dashboard of your CARDS app. Under settings, click cancel subscription.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, your data is safe. Collected data is yours and is never used or shared in any way shape or form.
